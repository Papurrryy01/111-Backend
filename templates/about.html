Our goal

Make expense tracking friendly for beginners: fast to add an expense, clear to read totals, and easy to keep categories consistent.

  • Log expenses with title, amount, date, and category.
  • Keep notes on purchases so you remember the context.
  • Review spending trends without complex reports.
How it helps you

Stay on top of your budget with a few habits:

  1. Add expenses the day you spend.
  2. Use consistent categories (Food, Transport, Bills).
  3. Check weekly to spot where to cut back.
